Strategic Timeline

A year-by-year breakdown of critical milestones and strategic priorities for your college admissions journey.

9th Grade - Foundation

Build Your Base Camp

  • Establish strong study habits and time management
  • Take challenging courses and maintain high GPA
  • Explore diverse extracurricular activities
  • Begin developing leadership skills
  • Take PSAT 8/9 for baseline assessment
10th Grade - Exploration

Find Your Path

  • Narrow extracurricular focus to 2-3 passion areas
  • Take PSAT/NMSQT (National Merit consideration)
  • Begin advanced coursework (AP/IB/Dual Enrollment)
  • Develop meaningful relationships with teachers
  • Start exploring potential college majors
  • Consider summer programs at target universities
11th Grade - Acceleration

Climb with Intensity

  • Take SAT/ACT (aim for top 1% scores)
  • Maximize AP course load while maintaining excellence
  • Pursue leadership positions in key activities
  • Begin college research and campus visits
  • Develop relationships with guidance counselors
  • Start scholarship research and applications
12th Grade - Summit Push

Reach the Peak

  • Complete and submit all college applications
  • Write compelling personal essays
  • Secure strong letters of recommendation
  • Apply for financial aid and scholarships
  • Prepare for college interviews
  • Make final college decision and prepare for transition
