Our Story

Founded by a team of Ivy League graduates and admissions experts, The Ascent Platform was born from a simple observation: the most successful college applicants aren't necessarily the smartest, but those who understand the strategic elements that make applications truly compelling.

After helping hundreds of students navigate the increasingly competitive landscape of elite college admissions, we realized the need for a systematic, comprehensive approach that could be scaled to help more exceptional students achieve their dreams.

Today, we're proud to have guided thousands of students to acceptances at the world's most prestigious institutions, with many receiving full scholarships and going on to become leaders in their chosen fields.
