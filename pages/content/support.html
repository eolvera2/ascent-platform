Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions

What services does The Ascent Platform offer? ▼

We offer comprehensive college consulting services including academic planning, application guidance, test prep support, essay assistance, interview preparation, and scholarship guidance. Our services are tailored to help students gain admission to elite universities.

How do I create an account and get started? ▼

You can create an account by clicking "Start Your Journey" on our homepage. Fill out the registration form with your information, select your goals, and we'll guide you through the onboarding process. You can also schedule a free consultation to discuss your needs.

When should I start working with The Ascent Platform? ▼

The earlier you start, the better prepared you'll be. We recommend beginning in 9th or 10th grade for comprehensive planning, but we can help students at any stage of their high school journey, including seniors who need immediate application support.

What are your pricing plans and payment options? ▼

We offer various service packages ranging from basic guidance to comprehensive support programs. Pricing varies based on services selected and duration of support. We accept major credit cards and offer payment plans. Contact us for detailed pricing information.

Do you guarantee admission to specific colleges? ▼

While we cannot guarantee admission to specific institutions due to the competitive nature of elite college admissions, our track record shows that 98% of our students gain admission to their target tier of universities. We focus on maximizing your chances of success.

I'm having trouble accessing my account. What should I do? ▼

Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If you continue to have issues, contact our technical support team at support@ascentplatform.com or use the live chat feature for immediate assistance.

How do you help with college essays and personal statements? ▼

Our essay coaching includes brainstorming sessions, outline development, draft reviews, and editing support. We help you craft compelling narratives that showcase your unique qualities and experiences while maintaining your authentic voice.
